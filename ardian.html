ardian powell's puzzles at vox were controversial (and some might say sloppily made), but some solvers found a way to make things a little more interesting.



THE ARDIAN POWELL BAR GAME

Take a shot every time...

...you have to translate an answer into Spanish before putting it in the grid
...a clue has a factual error
...an answer contains the letter C
...you have to put an answer in backwards
...there's a picture clue that doesn't work
...an answer is used as part of its own clue
...there's a Cockney clue
...an answer is five letters long
...a clue uses profanity
...a clue tries to coin the slang phrase "gamer yum-yums"
...the clue asks for a singular when the answer is a plural, or vice-versa
...part of a clue is clearly copy-pasted from the first paragraph of a Wikipedia article
...ENEMAS is an answer
...there's a fill-in-the-blank clue
...there's a cryptic clue

Just keep track of how many shots you take for each rule, and you should have everything squared away.
